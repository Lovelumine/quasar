Objectives of the RNAi Pesticide Database

To establish the first comprehensive database dedicated to RNAi-based pesticides, systematically compiling all information on RNA pesticides leveraging RNAi mechanisms. This will address the current fragmentation of research data, create a structured knowledge repository, and serve as a training dataset for AI-driven tools to automate the design of highly efficient dsRNA sequences.To provide researchers with user-friendly data query and analysis tools, enabling rapid screening of high-efficacy lethal gene targets and facilitating the design of multi-target fusion RNA pesticides, thereby accelerating RNA pesticide development. To collect field trial data on RNA pesticides, including stability, effective concentration, and other critical parameters, to support experimental validation and field application. To visualize dsRNA secondary structures using RNAfold, offering spatial structural insights to guide the engineering and optimization of dsRNA molecules. To develop novel strategies for pest resistance management by promoting the rational use of RNA pesticides, delaying the emergence of pest resistance, and ensuring sustainable agricultural production. To build an open-access platform fostering data sharing and collaboration among research teams, driving innovation in RNAi technology for pest control. To expand beyond agricultural pest management, with future applications in plant virus and pathogen management, supporting the broader implementation of RNAi technology across "macro-agricultural" scenarios.
